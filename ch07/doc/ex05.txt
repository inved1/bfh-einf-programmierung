Continue implementing your Panini simulation. Define corresponding classes for 
- children, 
- the schoolyard.

The main public methods of a child should do the following:
- receive some pocket money
- buy a picture package and add them to the album
- exchange duplicate pictures with another child
The exchanging of picture is the most complicated task, you may postpone it to a later moment

The main public methods of the schoolyard should do the following:
- let a new child enter the schoolyard
- let a child leave the schoolyard
- randomly pick a child to receive some pocket money
- randomly pick a child to buy a new picture package
- randomly pick two children to exchange their duplicates
If a child has filled up the album, it leaves the schoolyard.
